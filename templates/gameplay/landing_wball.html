About

Open wiffleball was started by a disgruntled wiffleball player, who was tired of the league elders taking game data and cooking up wild plots of their pitching data. This lead to many disputes, and equally wild accusations about league elders. The automation of the scoring process here aims to eliminate those kinds of problems.

Why Open?

As per the about there has been an increasing desire to keep the tallying of season stats as transparent as possible. Another reason is to invite others to contribute to the source code, especially with respect to graphing and analytics functionality, that can be used by all openwiffleball users to improve their scores.

Why Wiffleball?

If you really need to ask this question, we'd like to ask you to leave the site immediately. For those of you that didn't just listen to our instructions, we'll reward you with a history lesson. Wiffleball was founded originally by a group of union soldiers during the civil war in order to escape the harsh realities of war life. Eventually some conniving southern soldiers developed a knock-off game they called baseball. The relative success of baseball over wiffleball since then as a national past-time has since been a source of great discomfort to wiffleball enthusiasts to this day.
